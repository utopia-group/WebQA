<h2 class="entry-title">Philadelphia Medical Center</h2>
<time class="updated" datetime="2020-10-15T09:15:21-05:00">Oct 15, 2020</time>
</div>
</header>
<div class="entry-content">
<p>Carthage Area Hospital  assumed ownership and management of the Philadelphia
                                        Medical Center primary care clinic at 32787 U.S. Route 11 on April 23, 2018 and
                                        and re-opened on Monday, April 30 2018, offering patients access to a wider
                                        range of clinical services through its local network of care.</p>
<p>Longtime North Country Nurse Practitioner Dawn L. Scott, ANP-BC, PNP, is a member
                                        of the Carthage Area Hospital medical staff and provides primary care services
                                        at the clinic.</p>
<h3>Services:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Primary Medical Care</li>
<li>Sick visits</li>
<li>Blood Draw Station</li>
<li>Allergy Shots</li>
<li>Physicals</li>
</ul>
<h3>Providers:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Dawn L. Scott, ANP-BC, PNP</li>
</ul>
<h4>Most Insurances Accepted, including TRICARE, Martin’s Point, Medicaid, and
                                        Medicare.</h4>
<h3>Emergency number to call if facility is closed:</h3>
<p>Please call 911 or go to the nearest Emergency Room</p>

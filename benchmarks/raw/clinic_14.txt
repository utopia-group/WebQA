<div class="post-127 page type-page status-publish has-post-thumbnail hentry entry"><h1 class="entry-title">FAQ</h1>
<div class="entry-content"><h4 style="text-align: center;"> </h4>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #30b1d1;">What exactly is Advanced Urgent Care?</span>
</h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">As an emergency room alternative, <span style="color: #000000;"><strong>Advanced Urgent Care (AUC)</strong></span> stands
                            ready to meet your unscheduled medical needs. From minor problems such as cuts,
                            earaches and flu symptoms to more serious emergencies like abdominal pain, shortness of
                            breath and fever, we are here to help. Our experienced team of Emergency Physicians is
                            backed by an on-site lab, x-ray, ultrasound and CT scanner, so we can quickly, efficiently
                            and affordably treat most medical complications.</p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #30b1d1;">Do I need to make an appointment?</span></h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">No appointment is needed to be examined. However, the last
                            walk-in patient <strong>must arrive at least 30 minutes prior to closing</strong>. For
                            patients in need of <strong>diagnostic testing</strong> (e.g. CT scan, x-ray,
                            calcium scoring, lung cancer screening, ultrasound or blood work) please make an appointment
                            or have your referring physician call us ahead of time.  </p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #30b1d1;"><strong>What should I bring to my visit?</strong></span>
</h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">Please bring valid identification, your insurance card and any
                            pertinent medical records.</p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #1fb4c4;"><strong>Hablas español?</strong></span></h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">Si, hablamos español.</p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #30b1d1;">I don’t have a primary care doctor. Can I still come to AUC?</span></strong>
</h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">Absolutely. We are here for your urgent medical needs. </p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #30b1d1;">How do I know if I need the hospital ER or AUC?</span></strong>
</h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">If you feel that you are in life-threatening condition or in need
                            of an ambulance, please call 911. For virtually all other health issues, we can help.</p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #30b1d1;">What is your average wait time?</span></strong>
</h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">Unlike hospitals who can keep you waiting for hours, or primary
                            care physicians who can be booked for weeks, our average walk-in wait time is 25 minutes. </p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #30b1d1;">What can I do while I wait?</span></strong>
</h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">We proudly offer free Wi-Fi, live television, a substantial
                            magazine assortment as well as coloring pages for children. </p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #05bfed;">I arrived 35 minutes before closing and was turned away. Why is that?</span>
</h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">Since we are a walk-in clinic, we cannot predict the number of
                            patients who will visit us each day, nor the severity of their cases. If you arrive near
                            closing, and we are backed up, our policy is to have one of our medical staff assess the
                            urgency of your situation. Depending upon this evaluation, we may ask that you wait to be
                            seen, go to the ER or come back first thing tomorrow.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">While we would like to see <em>every</em> patient who walks into
                            our office each day, we simply cannot stay open past closing time for technical and safety
                            reasons. Therefore, we suggest that you arrive as early as possible. Please note that during
                            flu season, wait times can be longer than average. </p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The last patient is seen at the doctor’s discretion.</p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #30b1d1;">Do you have parking?</span></strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">Yes, we have sizable free parking lots at all locations.</p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #30b1d1;">Will my medical insurance be accepted?</span>
</h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">We accept most major insurance plans including: Medicare, Blue
                            Cross / Blue Shield, Cigna, Tricare, Aetna, United and Multiplan. We are sorry, but at this
                            time we do not accept Medicaid. </p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="color: #30b1d1;">How else can I pay?</span></strong>
</h4>
<p style="text-align: center;">We accept credit cards, debit cards and cash. For cash-paying
                            customers, we offer our services at a discounted rate <a href="http://www.urgentcarefloridakeys.com/resources/">(here)</a>. Our cash pricing
                            is also posted upon entry into our facility.  </p>

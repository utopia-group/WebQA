<p>The 26th International Symposium on High-Performance Computer Architecture provides a high-quality forum for scientists and engineers to present their latest research findings in this rapidly-changing field. </p>
<p><strong>HPCA-26 will be held at San Diego, CA on February 22-26, 2020.</strong></p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator is-style-wide"/>
<font color="red"><b>Congratulations to the following paper awards!</b></font><br/><br/>
<b>Best Paper Award:</b>
<ul>
<li><u>SIGMA: A Sparse and Irregular GEMM Accelerator with Flexible Interconnects for DNN Training</u><br/></li>
<i>Eric Qin, Ananda Samajdar, Hyoukjun Kwon, and Vineet Nadella (Georgia Institute of Technology); Sudarshan Srinivasan, Dipankar Das, and Bharat Kaul (Intel); Tushar Krishna (Georgia Institute of Technology)</i>
</ul>
<b>Best Paper Runner-Up Award:</b>
<ul>
<li><u>A Deep Reinforcement Learning Framework for Architectural Exploration: A Routerless NoC Case Study</u><br/></li>
<i>Ting-Ru Lin (University of Southern California); Drew Penney (Oregon State University); Massoud Pedram (University of Southern California); Lizhong Chen (Oregon State University)</i>
</ul>

<h1 class="entry-title">Call for Papers</h1> </header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<p>The IEEE International Symposium on High-Performance Computer Architecture provides a high-quality forum for scientists and engineers to present their latest research findings in this rapidly-changing field. Authors are invited to submit papers on all aspects of high-performance computer architecture.</p>
<p>Authors should submit an abstract by July 30, 2019 5:00pm Anywhere on Earth (AOE). They should submit the full version of the paper by August 6, 2019, 5:00pm AOE. No extensions will be granted. The full version should be a PDF file following the submission guidelines that will be made available at the submission website. Papers should be submitted for double-blind review. New-idea papers as well as papers that significantly advance established areas are strongly encouraged. Submission questions should be directed to Yan Solihin &lt;hpca2020pc@gmail.com&gt;. HPCA-26 will also host an Industrial Paper Session presenting novel insights from industry. See the HPCA 2020 website: TBD for additional information about HPCA 2020 and submission details.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.hpca-conf.org/2020/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/hpca20-cfp-v5.pdf">PDF Version</a></p>
<p>Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:</p>
<ul>
<li style="list-style-type: none;">
<ul>
<li>Accelerators</li>
<li>Architecture applications of Machine Learning</li>
<li>Caches</li>
<li>Cloud, datacenter, cluster/distributed systems</li>
<li>Embedded, IoT</li>
<li>Emerging technologies</li>
<li>FPGAs and Reconfigurable</li>
<li>GPUs</li>
<li>Hardware/software interactions/interface</li>
<li>ILP techniques (speculation, prediction, prefetching, etc.)</li>
<li>IO, storage</li>
<li>Memory – high level (VM/TLB, persistency, etc.)</li>
<li>Memory – low level (devices, organization, etc.)</li>
<li>Networking, interconnects</li>
<li>Parallel/multi-core architectures</li>
<li>Performance characterization/modeling</li>
<li>Power efficiency and management</li>
<li>Quantum architectures</li>
<li>Reliability and fault tolerance</li>
<li>Security</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>*** IMPORTANT DATES ***</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="list-style-type: none;">
<ul>
<li><span style="color: #800000;">Abstract submission : July 30, 2019</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #800000;">Full paper submission : August 6, 2019</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #800000;">Rebuttal/revision : <del>October 1-9, 2019 </del>October 17-20, 2019</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #800000;">Notification: November 6, 2019</span></li>
<li><span style="color: #800000;">Conference: Feb 22-26, 2020</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>ORGANIZERS</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="list-style-type: none;">
<ul>
<li>Program Chair: Prof. Yan Solihin (Univ. of Central Florida)</li>
<li>General Chairs: Prof. Dean Tullsen (UCSD) and Prof. Hadi Esmaeilzadeh (UCSD)</li>
<li>Web Chair: Prof. Jongse Park (KAIST)</li>
<li>Industry Session Chair: Dr. Alaa Alameldeen</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

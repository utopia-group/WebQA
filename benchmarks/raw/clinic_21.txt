<section id="CD9067B5-BBEE-4DD1-861F-EF9AC97A8A16">
<div class="panel panel-default col-md-12 clearfix">
<div class="panel-heading">
<h2 class="panel-title">
                            Services We Offer </h2>
<i class="fa fa-chevron-down"></i>
</div>
<div class="panelWrapper col-md-12 clearfix">
<div class="panel-body">
<div class="richtext-content">
<h3>Primary care</h3>
<p>Our providers care for your child's medical and developmental health needs, including
                                    the following: </p>
<ul>
<li>Well-child checkups</li>
<li>Sick visits for common illnesses or minor injuries</li>
<li>Routine school and sports physicals </li>
<li>Immunizations</li>
<li>Chronic illness care</li>
</ul>
<h3>Regular health checks</h3>
<p>Regular health checks or well-child checkups are recommended for all children. During
                                    a health check, your child will receive a physical exam, as well as any needed
                                    immunizations. Your child's provider will ask question about issues that might
                                    affect your child's health or performance in school. You will also have the chance
                                    to discuss your child's development, behavior concerns, nutrition, parenting and
                                    safety with your provider. We recommend scheduling your's child health checks
                                    according to the schedule below.</p>
<ul class="list-bulleted-col">
<li>Within the first seven days of being born</li>
<li>1 month old</li>
<li>2 months old</li>
<li>4 months old</li>
<li>6 months old</li>
<li>9 months old</li>
</ul>
<ul class="list-bulleted-col">
<li>12 months old</li>
<li>15 months old</li>
<li>18 months old</li>
<li>2 years old</li>
<li>2.5 years old</li>
<li>Annually from 3 to 20 years old</li>
</ul>
<h3>Sick visits</h3>
<p>We may be able to see your child the same day for sick visits. Same-day sick visits
                                    should only be used for minor illnesses or concerns such as colds and flu, earaches
                                    and ear infections, skin and scalp rashes, and stomachaches. It is best to call
                                    early in the morning for a same-day sick visit.</p>
</div>

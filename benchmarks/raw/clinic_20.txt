<h2>Choosing a Primary Care Physician</h2>
<p>When you choose the University of Chicago Medicine and UChicago Medicine Ingalls Memorial for
                            primary care, you are receiving expert care for preventive health and routine checkups. You
                            are also connecting with a world-renowned network of physician specialists who can help you
                            and your loved ones with any medical issues that arise.</p>
<p>Your primary care physician (PCP) is your main health care provider and is involved in your
                            short and long-term medical care. We have every type of primary care physician on staff:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/conditions-services/primary-care/physicians">Family Medicine Physicians</a>
</li>
<li><a href="/conditions-services/geriatrics/physicians">Geriatricians</a></li>
<li>
<a href="/conditions-services/obgyn/general-gynecology-well-woman-care">Gynecologists</a>
</li>
<li><a href="/conditions-services/primary-care/physicians">Internists</a></li>
<li><a href="/comer/conditions-services/general-pediatrics">Pediatricians</a></li>
</ul>
<p>Many of our clinics also employ nurse practitioners. As a result, you and your family can
                            receive the primary care services that each of you needs across your lifetimes within our
                            health system.</p>
<p>Our PCPs work side by side with any specialists you see to treat a disease or condition.
                            Together, we will effectively co-manage your health and ensure that you receive
                            comprehensive, coordinated care.</p>
<p>If you’re not sure which of our PCPs you should see, just ask. We can help you
                            determine which PCP is the best choice for you and your health issue.</p>
<h2>Primary Care Near Home</h2>
<p>With our ever-expanding network across the greater Chicago region, UChicago Medicine and
                            UChicago Medicine Ingalls Memorial offer several convenient locations for adult and
                            pediatric primary care.</p>
<p>We provide:</p>
<ul>
<li>Annual wellness exams and preventive screenings for men, women and children</li>
<li>Immunizations and developmental screenings for children</li>
<li>Onsite outpatient procedures and diagnostics</li>
<li>School and sports physicals</li>
<li>Care for urgent medical issues, such as upper respiratory infections, urinary tract
                                infections, sprains and bronchitis
                            </li>
<li>Care for common chronic conditions, such as diabetes, allergies, high blood pressure,
                                asthma, arthritis, anxiety and depression
                            </li>
</ul>
<p>Many of our clinics also have onsite lab testing (e.g., blood sugar readings, urine analysis)
                            so you can get your testing done at the same time you visit your physicians.</p>
<p>We recognize that some minor medical problems do not require an office visit. We encourage
                            our patients to take advantage of our <a href="/patients-visitors/patient-information/mychart/e-visits">e-visit</a> service
                            to diagnose and treat urinary tract infections, coughs and other minor illnesses.</p>

<h2>Virtual Video Visits</h2>
<p>
</p><p>Your family’s health doesn’t stop being a priority, even when it's a challenge to
                            leave the house. For your convenience and safety, we offer secure and easy virtual
                            visits by video for most of your healthcare needs. </p>
<p><strong>Current Patients </strong></p>
<p>Log in to your <a href="https://mychart.uchospitals.edu/mychart/Authentication/Login?" target="_blank">MyChart account</a> to choose from available
                            times to book your virtual appointment instantly with a primary care or specialty care
                            provider. </p>
<p><strong>New Patients </strong></p>
<p>Our real-time, <a href="https://mychart.uchospitals.edu/mychart/openscheduling">online
                            scheduling for video visits</a> lets you choose from available times so you can book
                            your virtual appointment instantly with a primary care or specialty care provider.

                                questionnaire</a></strong> (e-visit) to get a same-day diagnosis and treatment for
                                common conditions.</p>

<h2>Accepted Insurance Plans</h2>
<p>The University of Chicago Medicine participates in a variety of health insurance products and
                            plans. If UChicago Medicine is listed in your insurance company's network, you’ll be
                            able to receive care at our facilities with your “in-network” benefits. That
                            means your insurance company has agreed to accept certain negotiated rates, which can lower
                            your out-of-pockets costs. </p>
<p>If your insurance network does not include UChicago Medicine, but has out-of-network
                            benefits, you may have a higher out-of-pocket responsibility than if you received services
                            from an in-network provider.</p>
<p>Our list of accepted insurance providers is subject to change at any time. You should contact
                            your insurance company to confirm UChicago Medicine and UChicago Medicine Ingalls
                            participate in their network before scheduling your appointment. Your insurance company's
                            customer service representative can also help you verify your benefits, out-of-pocket costs
                            and coverage.</p>

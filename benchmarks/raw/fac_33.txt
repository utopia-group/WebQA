</p><p>
I am interested in the <b>foundations of software and system design</b>.
My research spans areas such as formal methods, computer-aided verification
and synthesis, safety-critical, embedded and cyber-physical systems, and security.
Our group develops theory and builds tools for designing better
(and better designing) systems.
</p>
<p>
I joined Northeastern in September 2018.

<h1 class="pageTitle">
<span class="titleText" id="dnn_ctr1053_dnnTITLE_titleLabel">Primary Care</span>
</h1>
<div class="clearfix"></div>
<div class="black_c" id="dnn_ctr1053_ContentPane"><!-- Start_Module_1053 -->
<div class="DNNModuleContent ModDNNHTMLC" id="dnn_ctr1053_ModuleContent">
<div class="Normal" id="dnn_ctr1053_HtmlModule_lblContent">
<p><span>When you’re choosing a primary care physician, you need an expert you can trust to give you excellent, personalized care, year after year. Experience the difference it makes to have a great doctor in a warm, inviting and convenient location right in Creve Coeur.</span>
</p>
<h2>Why Choose Barnes-Jewish West County Hospital for Primary
                                                        Care?</h2>
<p>You can rely on our expert internists to help keep you healthy
                                                        throughout your lifespan. We offer:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Expert physicians in west St. Louis County:</strong>
                                                            Our Washington University doctors and BJC Medical Group
                                                            physicians come from some of the region’s most
                                                            well-known groups in medicine. They’re known for care
                                                            with the very latest medical knowledge as its hallmark—coupled
                                                            with a great bedside manner to make your visits worthwhile.
                                                        </li>
<li><strong>Same-day sick appointments:</strong> If you’re
                                                            ill, you shouldn’t have to wait to see a doctor. If
                                                            you’re a current patient, you can call on us when you
                                                            need urgent care and we can often get you in that day to see
                                                            someone.
                                                        </li>
<li><strong>Tailored care for your unique needs:</strong> Our
                                                            physicians are not only diverse in age, gender and
                                                            ethnicity, they also have diverse areas of expertise in
                                                            quality healthcare that include women’s health, men’s
                                                            health, sports medicine, rheumatology (joint pain),
                                                            diabetes, disease prevention and weight loss.
                                                        </li>
<li><strong>Seamless referrals to expert
                                                            subspecialists:</strong> When you need to see a
                                                            subspecialist, you’ll have access to some of the
                                                            region’s top minds from BJC HealthCare, Washington
                                                            University Physicians and private practice physicians, many
                                                            of whom are located on our West County campus.
                                                        </li>
<li><strong>Personalized consultations on healthy
                                                            lifestyles:</strong> We offer wellness appointments for
                                                            personalized advice on nutrition, exercise, weight loss and
                                                            other ways you can prevent disease and live a longer,
                                                            healthier life.
                                                        </li>
</ul>
<h2>Primary Care at Barnes-Jewish West County Hospital: Our
                                                        Services</h2>
<p>Our caring, compassionate and knowledgeable physicians are
                                                        specially trained to respond to all your health and well-being
                                                        needs. Among the many treatments and services we offer, you can
                                                        come to us for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Check-ups
                                                            <ul>
<li>School, work and sports physicals</li>
<li>Vaccinations and immunizations</li>
<li>Wellness exams required by insurance</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Chronic conditions
                                                            <ul>
<li>
<a href="/Health-Library/View-Content?contentTypeId=85&amp;contentId=P00043">Arthritis</a>
</li>
<li>
<a href="/Health-Library/View-Content?contentTypeId=85&amp;contentId=P00816">Asthma</a>
</li>
<li>
<a href="/Health-Library/View-Content?contentTypeId=85&amp;contentId=P01513">Diabetes</a>
</li>
<li>
<a href="/Health-Library/View-Content?contentTypeId=85&amp;contentId=P00206">Heart
                                                                        failure</a></li>
<li>
<a href="/Health-Library/View-Content?contentTypeId=85&amp;contentId=P00224">Hypertension</a> (high
                                                                    blood pressure)
                                                                </li>
<li>High cholesterol</li>
<li>
<a href="/Health-Library/View-Content?contentTypeId=1&amp;contentId=3022">Joint
                                                                        pain</a></li>
<li>
<a href="/Health-Library/View-Content?ContentTypeID=85&amp;ContentID=P08231">Kidney
                                                                        disease</a></li>
<li>
<a href="/Medical-Services/Digestive-Diseases/Conditions-We-Treat/Liver-Disease">Liver
                                                                        disease</a></li>
<li>
<a href="/Health-Library/View-Content?contentTypeId=85&amp;contentId=P00437">Thyroid
                                                                        disorders</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Common illnesses
                                                            <ul>
<li>Dermatology, including mole, skin tag and wart
                                                                    removal
                                                                </li>
<li>Flu shots and diagnosis</li>
<li>Seasonal <a href="/Health-Library/View-Content?contentTypeId=85&amp;contentId=P09504">allergies</a>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Injuries and orthopedics
                                                            <ul>
<li>X-rays, MRIs and imaging</li>
<li>Physical therapy referrals</li>
<li>Back, hip, wrist and other kinds of pain</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Women’s health
                                                            <ul>
<li>Breast exams and cancer screenings</li>
<li>Contraception</li>
<li>
<a href="/Health-Library/View-Content?contentTypeId=1&amp;contentId=2027">Menopause</a>
                                                                    management, including hormonal and non-hormonal
                                                                    therapies
                                                                </li>
<li>Cervical cancer screening</li>
<li>Post-pregnancy care, including diabetes follow-up
                                                                </li>
<li>Osteoporosis management</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Men’s health
                                                            <ul>
<li>Enlarged prostate care</li>

</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h2>Meet Our Primary Care Physicians</h2>
<p>At Barnes-Jewish West County Hospital, all of our doctors take a
                                                        personal approach to your health and well-being. Our internal
                                                        medicine physicians, or “internists,” specialize in
                                                        treating adults. They are equipped to handle almost any problem
                                                        you present, from common ailments to complex medical conditions,
                                                        like diabetes, heart disease or chronic pain. They can also
                                                        refer you to additional specialists and will communicate with
                                                        them to coordinate your care.</p>
<p><strong>Internal medicine specialists on the campus of
                                                        Barnes-Jewish West County Hospital include:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<a href="https://doctors.bjc.org/wlp2/barnesjewishwestcounty/doctors/info/A5P000FL/Esther-F-Adade-MD">Esther

<a href="https://doctors.bjc.org/wlp2/barnesjewishwestcounty/doctors/info/AMP0000P/Matthew-A.-Beckerdite-MD?utm_medium=&amp;utm_source=link_BJWCH" target="_blank">Matthew Beckerdite, MD</a></li>
<li>

<h2>Preventive Medicine and Wellness</h2>
<p>You don’t have to be sick to have a strong relationship
                                                        with your primary care doctor. Our physicians have a special
                                                        interest in preventive medicine and will do everything in their
                                                        power to help you stay healthy.</p>
<p>We encourage you to make an annual wellness appointment (usually
                                                        covered by insurance) where you can discuss:</p>
<ul>
<li>Your unique risk factors for disease</li>
<li>Screenings and tests for cancer prevention</li>
<li>Ways to lose weight</li>
<li>Managing your health without medication</li>
<li>The best nutrition plan for your needs</li>
<li>Exercise regimens that fit your lifestyle</li>
<li>Tips for monitoring and improving your health</li>
</ul>

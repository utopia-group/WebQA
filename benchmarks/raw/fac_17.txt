<p>
I am interested in static analysis by the means of Abstract Interpretation:
</p><ul>
<li>static analysis of mobile systems (<a href="https://www.di.ens.fr/~feret/thesis">PhD</a>)</li>
<li>static analysis of critical embedded systems (<a href="http://www.astree.ens.fr">ASTREE analyzer</a>)</li>
<li>static analysis of biological networks (<a href="http://fontana.med.harvard.edu/www/Documents/Lab/research.signaling.htm">Executable Knowledge</a> / <a href="http://www.kappalanguage.org">Kappa language</a>)
</li></ul>
<h4><a name="research projects"></a>Research projects:</h4>
<ul>
<li>I am coordinating the ANR project <a href="anastasec">AnaStaSec</a>, on the analysis of security properties in critical embedded software.  </li>
<li>I lead the <a href="http://www.inria.fr/centre/paris">INRIA-Paris</a> partner team in the ANR project <a href="http://www.agence-nationale-recherche.fr/Project-ANR-18-CE25-0007">DCore</a>, on Causal debugging for concurrent systems.</li>
<li>I lead the <a href="http://www.inria.fr/centre/paris">INRIA-Paris </a> partner team in The <a href="https://itcancer.aviesan.fr">ITMO Cancer</a> project TGFSysBio 
coordinated by Nathalie Théret (<a href="https://www.irset.org/?lang=fr">IRSET (Université Rennes 1)</a>)
</li></ul>

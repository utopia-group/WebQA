In bridging this gap, the course will cover many subjects beginning with binary logic, combinatorial and sequential circuit design, memory structures, instruction set architectures, and, ultimately, basic processor design.<h2>Prerequisites</h2>An introductory programming course, such as COMS 1004 or 1007. You need to understand the basics of imperative, sequential programming to understand the assembly language programming we will discuss.<h2>Late Policy</h2>Each homework assignment is due in the CSEE 3827 box in the TA room by 5PM on the due date.

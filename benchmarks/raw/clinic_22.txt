<h2 class=""><strong>Primary Care Services</strong></h2>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-sm-8">
<p><strong></strong>Primary care physicians (PCP) at UI Health provide the care
                                        every member of your family needs at each stage of life.<br/><br/>Our primary care
                                        doctors perform routine health exams, such as physical exams, <a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/pediatrics/childrens-hospital/our-services/pediatric-primary-care">well-child
                                            visits</a>, and <a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/obstetrics-and-gynecology/our-obgyn-services/general-obstetrics-and-gynecology">well-woman
                                            visits</a>; help you prevent or manage of chronic conditions like <a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/pediatrics/childrens-hospital/our-services/pediatric-endocrinology/for-patients/diabetes">diabetes</a>,
                                        <a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/cardiology">high blood pressure</a>, and <a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/pulmonary/our-areas-of-expertise/asthma">asthma</a>;
                                        and provide education about exercising, eating healthy, and other important
                                        steps you can take to be your healthiest self.<br/><br/>Partnering with a UI
                                        Health PCP is one of the most important things you can do for your health.
                                        <br/><br/></p>
</div>

<h3>The Care You Need in a Way That Works for You</h3>
<p>With <a href="/patients-and-visitors/patient-information/mychart/virtual-visits-with-mychart">virtual
                                        visits</a>, seeing your UI Health provider is as simple as clicking a link in a
                                        text message. It’s never been more convenient to get the care you need to
                                        stay healthy.</p>

<div>
<h3>Preventative Care for the Whole Family</h3>
                                At UI Health, we believe in preventive care for the whole family, from children to older
                                adults. <br/>Find the PCP that’s right for you.<br/><br/>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-xs-6 col-md-4">
<h4><a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/internal-medicine">Internal
                                            Medicine</a></h4>
                                        Internal medicine physicians specialize in the prevention, diagnosis, and
                                        treatment of health issues that impact adults.
                                    </div>
<div class="col-xs-6 col-md-4">
<h4><a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/family-medicine">Family Medicine</a>
</h4>
                                        Family medicine doctors diagnose, treat, and help prevent disease for patients
                                        of all ages, from children to older adults.
                                    </div>
<div class="col-xs-6 col-md-4">
<h4><a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/pediatrics/childrens-hospital">Pediatrics</a>
</h4>
                                        Our general pediatricians provide preventative care that ensures children grow
                                        up healthy and develop properly.
                                    </div>
</div>
<hr/>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-xs-6 col-md-4">
<h4><a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/obstetrics-and-gynecology">OB/GYN</a>
</h4>
                                        Our team provides a variety of services for obstetric and gynecologic
                                        healthcare, including well-woman exams, family planning advice, prenatal care, and
                                        more.
                                    </div>
<div class="col-xs-6 col-md-4">
<h4>Two-Generation Clinic</h4>
                                        The Two-Generation Clinic at UI Health allows women and children to receive all
                                        of the primary care services they need at the same place and time.
                                    </div>
<div class="col-xs-6 col-md-4">
<h4><a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/geriatrics">Geriatric Medicine</a></h4>
                                        Our comprehensive team ensures older adults and their families have the
                                        education, support, and guidance they need to develop personalized care plans.
                                    </div>

<h2 class=""><strong>Our Services</strong></h2>
<p>Our primary care providers (PCP) act as your personal healthcare provider. Our experts
                                from Family Medicine, Internal Medicine, Obstetrics &amp; Gynecology, and Pediatrics
                                treat patients of all ages, and we work together to manage all your family's healthcare
                                needs, from treating common medical problems to caring for chronic medical
                                conditions.</p>
<h3><a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/family-medicine">Family Medicine</a></h3>
<p>Our family medicine doctors take care of all your medicine needs: We provide a broad
                                range of primary care services, including women's healthcare, immunizations, physicals,
                                lab testing, medication refill, follow-up visits, and health counseling and education.
                                We also have a <a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/family-medicine/physician-home-visits">Home
                                    Visits Program</a> to provide medical care to patients of all ages without the need
                                for hospitalization or an emergency visit. </p>
<h3><a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/internal-medicine">Internal Medicine</a></h3>
<p>The internal medicine specialists at UI Health are committed to helping you be the
                                healthiest version of you. Our doctors focus on primary care, health maintenance,
                                disease prevention and risk factors, and specialty care to provide you a personalized
                                healthcare plan that fits your needs.</p>
<h3><a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/obstetrics-and-gynecology">Obstetrics &amp;
                                Gynecology</a></h3>
<p>Our board-certified obstetricians and gynecologists focus on providing quality healthcare
                                to girls, women, and expectant mothers. Whether you need a routine well-woman exam,
                                family planning advice, or help with a gynecological condition, our OB/GYN team provides
                                offer quality healthcare for women throughout their reproductive years and beyond.</p>
<h3><a href="/primary-and-specialty-care/pediatrics/childrens-hospital">Pediatrics</a></h3>
<p>The Department of Pediatrics delivers compassionate, personalized care to children of all
                                ages, from infancy into young adulthood. We bring together primary and specialty
                                pediatric care in one location. From well-child visits and immunizations in our clinics,
                                to advanced care and emergency services on our designated pediatric hospital floor, we
                                work with your family to address your medical and emotional concerns while providing the
                                best care to your child. Our team helps manage chronic medical conditions and diseases
                                to ensure your child grows up healthy and develops properly.</p>

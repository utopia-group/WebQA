<p style="text-align:justify;"> My research interests span a range of areas
        in Programming Languages,
        Operating Systems, and Blockchain Systems,
        with a focus on language-based support for safety
              and security, certified system software, certified programming and
              compilation, formal methods, and concurrency reasoning.
      </p>
<p style="text-align:justify;">
        I co-founded
        <a href="https://certik.org">CertiK</a>,
        a systems verification startup.
        Press on CertiK:
        <a href="https://www.forbes.com/sites/shermanlee/2018/07/10/blockchain-smart-contracts-more-trouble-than-they-are-worth/#2287e96d23a6">Forbes</a>,
        <a href="https://www.coindesk.com/binance-labs-invests-millions-in-blockchain-auditing-platform-certik">Coindesk</a>,
        <a href="https://ocr.yale.edu/news/yale-technology-could-fix-blockchain-s-security-issues-and-make-it-lot-more-viable">Yale News</a>.

      </p>

<section class="container bio">
<p>I am a tenure-track faculty at <a href="http://www.mpi-sws.org">MPI-SWS</a>. Before that, I did my PhD
    with Viktor Kuncak at <a href="http://www.epfl.ch">EPFL</a>.
    I am generally interested in programming languages, software verification and approximate computing.
    Currently, my focus lies on applications in numerical, embedded or cyber-physical domains.
    </p>

<div class="sqs-block-content"><h1 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Meet Our
                                        Doctors</h1>
<h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">GENTLE RESPECT. GENUINE WARMTH.</h2>
<h3 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">From Skilled and Experienced Doctors</h3>
<p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">When your back hurts, our caring team
                                            is here for you.</p>
<p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Our experienced and highly trained
                                            doctors are here to help you experience relief. They know that when you are
                                            in pain, you need more than just a doctor, you need someone who listens and
                                            cares. They also know that you want to be involved in your care – to know
                                            what is going on, to receive your diagnosis on a timely basis and be given
                                            clear goals for your ideal outcome. Our care is extremely personalized – you
                                            will see only one doctor and one surgeon throughout your time with us. Isn’t
                                            that the kind of care you deserve? We think so.</p></div>

<div class="sqs-block-content"><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">NILESH
                                                PATEL, M.D.</h2>
<h3 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><em>Spine Surgeon, Board Certified,
                                                    Assistant Professor of Orthopedic Surgery Wayne State University.
                                                    Alpha Omega Alpha</em></h3>
<p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Dr. Patel grew up in
                                                    southeastern Michigan and had returned to the Detroit area after
                                                    completing his training. He attended Northeastern Ohio Universities
                                                    College of Medicine and completed his residency at Summa Health
                                                    System in Akron, OH. Further subspecialty training was undertaken
                                                    during a Spine Surgery Fellowship at New England Baptist Hospital in
                                                    Boston.</p>
<p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">His focus is on adult spinal
                                                    disorders, including the treatment of cervical, thoracic and lumbar
                                                    spine pathology. Dr. Patel has a special interest in minimally
                                                    invasive techniques and travels across the country training other
                                                    surgeons on these new technologies. Some of these treatments include
                                                    Microdiscectomies, Minimally Invasive Fusions, including the cutting
                                                    edge and highly effective iFuse implant system, M.I.L.D.,
                                                    Kyphoplasty and many more.</p>
<p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">He is involved with the latest
                                                    research and teaches as an Assistant Professor in the Department of
                                                    Orthopedics at Wayne State University. Additionally, he is the
                                                    Director of Spine Surgery at Oakwood Healthcare System.</p>
<p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Dr. Patel’s goal is to not
                                                    only improve your pain and quality of life but to do it in the
                                                    quickest, least painful way possible. He will strive not only to
                                                    treat your condition but to also educate you so you can be an
                                                    informed patient.</p></div>

<div class="sqs-block-content"><h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">RAZMIG
                                                HALADJIAN, M.D.</h2>
<h3 style="white-space:pre-wrap;"><em>Board Certified Pain Medicine
                                                    Specialist Diplomate, American Board of Anesthesiology</em></h3>
<p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Dr. Razmig Haladjian, a native
                                                    of Beirut, Lebanon and a graduate of the American University of
                                                    Beirut School of Medicine, completed his residency in Anesthesiology
                                                    at Wayne State University School of Medicine. He performed his
                                                    fellowship in Pain Management at the University of Michigan in Ann
                                                    Arbor. Dr. Haladjian employs a multi-disciplinary approach in
                                                    solving patients’ chronic pain issues with a strong emphasis in
                                                    cutting-edge interventional procedures. Dr. Haladjian has special
                                                    interest and expertise in treating spinal conditions such as
                                                    compression fractures, spinal stenosis, and failed back surgery
                                                    (post-laminectomy syndrome). Through his extensive training and his
                                                    years of experience as a pain management physician, Dr. Haladjian
                                                    has mastered techniques ranging from simple spinal interventions
                                                    such as epidurals and facet blocks to more advanced procedures such
                                                    as kyphoplasties, spinal cord stimulations, and minimally invasive
                                                    spine surgeries.</p>
<p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Dr. Haladjian places extreme
                                                    importance in identifying the pain generator in order to recommend
                                                    the appropriate treatment. In addressing the pharmacological aspect
                                                    of pain, Dr. Haladjian utilizes combinations of pain medications as
                                                    well as adjuvant medications to limit side effects to treat chronic
                                                    pain conditions. Dr. Haladjian has acquired an extensive amount of
                                                    experience in treating cancer related pain conditions. Through his
                                                    use of a vast store of treatment modalities he is able to
                                                    successfully relieve cancer pain. He is also trained in
                                                    detoxification from pain medications.</p>
<p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Dr. Haladjian is a member of
                                                    the International Spine Intervention Society (SIS). He continues to
                                                    be very active in pursuing new treatments that could be beneficial
                                                    to his patients. Dr. Haladjian has been a keynote speaker at medical
                                                    meetings. He has authored and co-authored pain related articles.</p>
<p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Along with his colleagues, Dr.
                                                    Haladjian strives to offer excellent care through careful symptom
                                                    assessment and use of advanced treatment options and continues to
                                                    remain on the forefront of pain medicine breakthroughs.</p></div>

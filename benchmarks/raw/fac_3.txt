<h1><strong>Ori Lahav / אורי להב</strong></h1>
<p style="max-width: 70em; text-align: justify">
I am a faculty member in the 
<a href="https://en.cs.tau.ac.il/computer">School of Computer Science</a>
at <a href="https://english.tau.ac.il/">Tel Aviv University</a>.
 I did my PhD at Tel Aviv University under the supervision of <a href="http://www.cs.tau.ac.il/~aa/">Arnon Avron</a>.
 In 2014, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Tel Aviv University hosted by <a href="http://www.cs.tau.ac.il/~msagiv/">Mooly Sagiv</a>.
 After that, until September 2017, I was a postdoctoral researcher at <a href="http://www.mpi-sws.org/">MPI-SWS</a> in Germany hosted by <a href="https://www.mpi-sws.org/~viktor/">Viktor Vafeiadis</a> and <a href="https://www.mpi-sws.org/~dreyer/">Derek Dreyer</a>.
<br/><br/>My main areas of research are programming languages and verification, with a
focus on concurrency and relaxed memory models.
I am also interested in proof-theory and semantics of (non-classical) logics
and automated reasoning.
</p>
<p style="max-width: 70em; text-align: justify">
<br/><br/><br/><span style="color: #800000;"><strong>I am looking for highly motivated students (PhD and MSc) and postdocs interested in programming languages theory, concurrency, logics for proving correctness of programs and other formal methods. If interested, please send me an email!
<br/> <br/></strong></span>
</p>

<p>Georgia Clinic, PC has been caring for Families of Metro Atlanta since 1999. Our State of the
                            Art Medical Offices are staffed with over twenty American Board-Certified Physicians and
                            Nurse Practitioner/Physician Assistant's including Family Medicine/Primary Care, and
                            Specialty Physicians. Our physicians and staff are available whenever you and your Family
                            are in need of Quality Healthcare in 16 locations all throughout Metropolitan Atlanta.</p>
<img alt="Main Office" class="img_content_left" src="/assets/silver_websites/georgia-clinic/office.jpg"/>
<p>Our dedicated Physicians and Staff work together as a team to Provide High-Quality
                            Comprehensive Care. Our goal is to provide each and every patient with individualized
                            medical care to meet their needs. We strive to Provide Acute Care (non-life-threatening), as
                            well as Preventative Care thereby improving the quality of life for you, our Patients. We
                            have 16 convenient locations and are open 7 days a week including Weekends and Holidays. We
                            accommodate Walk-in and appointment schedule Patients.</p>
<p>Thank you for considering our practice as a Home for your Medical Care. Welcome to Georgia
                            Clinic, we look forward to serving all your Healthcare Needs.</p>
<p>For life-threatening emergencies please seek hospital care and/or call 911.<br/>

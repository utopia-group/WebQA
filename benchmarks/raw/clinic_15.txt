<h1 class="entry-title">Services: Urgent Care / Work Injuries / Occupational
                                        Medicine/ Auto Injuries</h1>

</header><!-- .entry-header -->
<div class="entry-content">
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<p>When you suddenly feel unwell, all you can think of is ‘Where to get urgent
                                            care near me?’. Our walk-in clinic provides great services for our patients.
                                            If your primary doctor is unable to see you, you’re welcome to visit our
                                            clinic for care. At Doctor’s Urgent Care, we provide high-quality,
                                            professional and affordable care that you will greatly appreciate. Also,
                                            most personal insurance is accepted, in addition to all workers’
                                            compensation and motor vehicle accidents insurance.</p>
<p>The services we provide include, but are not limited to, the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>All types of sick visits with day, evening and weekend hours</li>
<li>Rapid walk-in, comprehensive Urgent Care, Work Injury, Occupational
                                                Medicine or Auto Injury services
                                            </li>
<li>We are certified and well trained in Occupational and Work Injury care
                                            </li>
<li>In-house lab work – includes <a href="/care-services-offered/rapid-strep-testing-strep-throat.html">strep
                                                test</a>, <a href="/care-services-offered/urine-pregnancy-tests-vs-blood-pregnancy-tests.html">pregnancy
                                                tests (blood and urine)</a>, <a href="/care-services-offered/mono-testing.html">mono testing</a>, <a href="/care-services-offered/urinalysis.html">urinalysis</a>, <a href="/care-services-offered/urine-drug-screen.html">urine drug
                                                screening via chain of custody</a>, <a href="/care-services-offered/breath-alcohol-test-bat.html">Breath
                                                Alcohol Test (BAT)</a></li>
<li>Sending out labs – including those of a typical lab draw station does
                                                (Quest or Labcorp)
                                            </li>
<li><a href="/care-services-offered/x-rays.html">X-Rays</a> on-site</li>
<li>Vaccines (<a href="/care-services-offered/flu-vaccination.html">Flu</a>,
                                                <a href="/care-services-offered/tetanus-injection.html">Tetanus</a>, <a href="/care-services-offered/hepatitis-b-vaccine.html">Hepatitis
                                                    B</a>) and vitamin (<a href="/care-services-offered/b12-injection.html">vitamin B12</a>)
                                                injections
                                            </li>
<li>Crash cart, AED and oxygen on-site</li>
<li>Allergy treatment</li>
<li>Eye exams with Fluorescein</li>
<li>Injection of joints with steroids</li>
<li>Full line of injection medications – including antibiotics, anti-nausea
                                                medications <strong>(no narcotics or controlled substances).</strong>
</li>
<li><a href="/care-services-offered/ekg.html">EKG</a></li>
<li><a href="/care-services-offered/nebulizer-treatment.html">Nebulizer
                                                treatment</a></li>
<li>Various types of biopsies</li>
<li><a href="/care-services-offered/laceration-repair.html">Laceration
                                                Repair</a></li>
<li>Minor surgeries – includes <a href="/care-services-offered/abscess-drainage.html">abscess
                                                drainage</a>, <a href="/care-services-offered/ingrown-toenail.html">ingrown
                                                toenail</a>, <a href="/care-services-offered/foreign-body-removal.html">foreign
                                                body removal</a>, surgical removal of skin lesions, etc.
                                            </li>
<li><a href="/care-services-offered/splinting-of-fractures.html">Splinting
                                                of Fractures</a></li>
<li><a href="/care-services-offered/ace-wrap.html">Ace Wrapping</a></li>
<li><a href="/care-services-offered/joint-aspiration.html">Aspiration of
                                                Joints</a></li>
<li><a href="/care-services-offered/free-medications.html">Free antibiotic
                                                pills with office visit</a></li>
</ul>
<p>Our walk in clinic provides a wide range of urgent care services on evenings
                                            and weekends. Visit Doctors Urgent Care for all above mentioned procedures
                                            as well as for any non-life-threatening condition. </p>
</div>

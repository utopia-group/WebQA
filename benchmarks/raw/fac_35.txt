            <div class="title">
                <h2>About me</h2>
            <p>I am a professor in the 
                 <a href="http://automataseminar.wikispaces.com/">Automata theory group</a>
                 at the <A HREF="http://www.mimuw.edu.pl/english">Faculty of Mathematics, Informatics and Mechanics</A>,
                 <A href="http://www.uw.edu.pl">University of Warsaw</a>.</p>
                 <p>My research areas are: &nbsp; automata theory, concurrency theory, formal verification, systems biology.
                 <p>Here is <a href="http://atoms.mimuw.edu.pl">the blog on computation with atoms</a>. 
            </p>

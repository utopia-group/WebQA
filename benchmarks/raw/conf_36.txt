<p>
        The International Symposium on Computer Architecture is the premier 
        forum for new ideas and experimental results in computer architecture. 
        The conference specifically seeks particularly forward-looking and 
        novel submissions.
      </p>
<p>
        In 2020, the 47<sup>th</sup> edition of ISCA will be held in
        Valencia, Spain.
      </p>
<p>
<a href="submit/guidelines.html"><span class="glyphicon glyphicon-info-sign"></span> Submission Guidelines</a>

<h3>Topics</h3>
      Papers are solicited on a broad range of topics, including (but not limited to):
      <ul>
<li>Processor, memory, and storage systems architecture</li>
<li>Multiprocessor systems</li>
<li>Datacenter-scale computing</li>
<li>IoT, mobile and embedded architecture</li>
<li>Interconnection network, router and network interface architecture</li>
<li>Accelerator-based, application-specific and reconfigurable architecture</li>
<li>Architectural support for programming languages or software development</li>
<li>Architectural support for security or virtualization</li>
<li>Instruction, thread and data-level parallelism</li>
<li>Dependable processor and system architecture</li>
<li>Architecture for emerging technologies and applications, including machine learning, novel memory technologies, quantum computing, etc.</li>
<li>Effects of circuits or technology on architecture</li>
<li>Architecture modeling and simulation methodologies</li>
<li>Evaluation and measurement of real computing systems</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<hr/>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-xs-12">
<h3>Industry Track</h3>
<p>
        This year, ISCA has an <a href="submit/industry.html">industry track</a>
        for submissions that provide retrospective evaluations of real working
        products, or of planned products that were cancelled but present 
        interesting insights or lessons learned.
        Note that the industry track is not meant for student intern
        projects, and any such papers will be rejected without consideration.
        Industry track papers are due in January 2020.
        See the <a href="submit/industry.html">industry track page</a> for
        more details.
      </p>

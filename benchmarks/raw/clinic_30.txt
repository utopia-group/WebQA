<h3>Carient Heart and Vascular physicians
                                                                                have helped improve the heart-health of
                                                                                cardiovascular patients for more than 20
                                                                                years in Northern Virginia.</h3>
<p>Carient physicians are some of the most
                                                                                respected cardiologist’s in the state of
                                                                                Virginia. Our physicians are board
                                                                                certified and have active privileges at
                                                                                all hospitals throughout Northern
                                                                                Virginia. Our physicians have continued
                                                                                training to add subspecialties in
                                                                                Interventional Cardiology, Nuclear
                                                                                Cardiology, Echocardiography, Clinical
                                                                                Cardiac Electrophysiology, Clinical
                                                                                Research, and Peripheral Vascular
                                                                                Disease. We are experts across all
                                                                                specialty areas, leaders in cutting edge
                                                                                research, and are ready to address all
                                                                                of your cardiovascular needs.</p>

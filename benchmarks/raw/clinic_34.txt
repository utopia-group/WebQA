<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--text-with-heading paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h2 class="field field--name-field-header field--type-string field--label-hidden field__item">
                                                                            Health Insurance Requirements</h2>
<div class="field field--name-field-text-section field--type-text-long field--label-hidden field__item">
<h3>University Student Insurance</h3>
<p>Boynton Health welcomes <a data-entity-substitution="canonical" data-entity-type="node" data-entity-uuid="4efa886f-5526-47a0-ae8d-b28fa4efa31c" href="/patient-focus/students" title="Students">University
                                                                                students</a>.</p>
<p>The University of Minnesota requires
                                                                                students to have health insurance. If
                                                                                you do not have insurance, you may be
                                                                                able to buy coverage from the Office of
                                                                                Student Health Benefits.</p>
<p>See details on the <a href="https://shb.umn.edu/" target="_blank">Office of Student
                                                                                Health Benefits website</a>.</p>
<h3>University Staff and Faculty
                                                                                Insurance</h3>
<p>Boynton Health welcomes <a data-entity-substitution="canonical" data-entity-type="node" data-entity-uuid="4826378a-853d-4acf-8af2-ce53abb8506e" href="/patient-focus/faculty-and-staff" title="Faculty and Staff">UPlan
                                                                                members</a>.</p>
<p>We take part in most UPlan health plans.
                                                                                UPlan members may be able to choose
                                                                                Boynton as their primary care clinic.
                                                                                Our Gopher Quick Clinic is open to most
                                                                                UPlan members with copayment, consult
                                                                                your insurance card for details.</p>
<p>Flu shots, health coaching and health
                                                                                screening are offered to UPlan members
                                                                                at no out-of-pocket cost. These benefits
                                                                                also earn Wellness Program points that
                                                                                can lower your UPlan premiums.</p>
<p>See details about the UPlan on the <a href="https://humanresources.umn.edu/" target="_blank">Office of Human
                                                                                Resources website</a>.</p></div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="field field--name-field-general-page-top field--type-entity-reference-revisions field--label-hidden field__item">
<div class="paragraph paragraph--type--text-with-heading paragraph--view-mode--default">
<h2 class="field field--name-field-header field--type-string field--label-hidden field__item">
                                                                            Billing and Student Services Fee</h2>
<div class="field field--name-field-text-section field--type-text-long field--label-hidden field__item">
<p>If you have any questions about a bill,
                                                                                call our patient accounting department,
                                                                                612-624-6985, or see <a data-entity-substitution="canonical" data-entity-type="node" data-entity-uuid="840d3b3a-b724-4004-9902-ed954009650d" href="/billing" title="Billing">billing
                                                                                    information</a>.</p>
<p>If you are a student who pays the Student
                                                                                Services Fee, the amount of your bill
                                                                                not covered by insurance will be paid
                                                                                for from the Student Services Fee—some
                                                                                exceptions apply.</p>
<p>See <a data-entity-substitution="canonical" data-entity-type="node" data-entity-uuid="7d85bcd7-3745-4c7f-bd05-f6e339a3a981" href="/ssf" title="Student Services Fee">Student
                                                                                Services Fee</a> details.</p>
<p> </p></div>

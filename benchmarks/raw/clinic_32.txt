                                                                    also provides lab and STD testing, occupational
                                                                    medicine, physicals for sports and employment and
                                                                    travel medicine such as vaccines when leaving the
                                                                    country.</p>
<h2>Get the Care You Need Right Now</h2>
<ul>
<li>Illness and Infection.</li>
<li>Fractures and Sprains.</li>
<li>Lab Testing and Screening.</li>
<li>Injury and Minor Trauma.</li>
</ul>
<p><span>AFC Urgent Care</span> provides high-quality,
                                                                    urgent care at a low cost! When you come to <span>AFC Urgent Care</span> you’ll
                                                                    be seen by a highly qualified provider every time.
                                                                    Our professional staff of providers, x-ray
                                                                    technicians and medical assistants are here to not
                                                                    only provide you with high quality care but a
                                                                    positive experience. We have a comfortable lounge to
                                                                    wait in, a very friendly and professional staff and
                                                                    state of the art equipment (including digital x-ray
                                                                    and a full lab) to take of all your urgent care
                                                                    needs.</p>
<ul>
<li>No Appointment Needed.</li>
<li>X-ray and Lab Services On Site.</li>
<li>Vaccinations and Physicals.</li>
<li>Major Insurance Plans Accepted.</li>
</ul>
<p>Open to the public with extended hours each day,
                                                                    <span>AFC Urgent Care</span> is convenient and
                                                                    reliable for those in need. Urgent care services
                                                                    cover a wide area and a variety of medical needs.
                                                                    Instead of scheduling an appointment at your local
                                                                    clinic, skip the hassle and visit <span>AFC Urgent Care</span> today.

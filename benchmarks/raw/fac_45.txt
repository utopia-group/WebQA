<p style="width:60%">
Our research broadly spans computer architecture, compilers and computer systems. We embrace the <a href="https://www.cs.utexas.edu/users/EWD/transcriptions/EWD02xx/EWD209.html">constructive</a>, <a href="https://cacm.acm.org/magazines/2015/4/184705-who-builds-a-house-without-drawing-blueprints/fulltext">specification-directed</a> approach to system design where possible. 
We've found the approach not only leads to correctness but also to efficiency. 
</p>
<p>

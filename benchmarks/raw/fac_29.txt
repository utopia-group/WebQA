<div class="container">
<p>

      I am a Professor in
      the <a href="http://www.cs.tufts.edu">Department of Computer
      Science</a> at <a href="http://www.tufts.edu">Tufts
      University</a>. The goal of my research is to develop
      fundamental new ways to make it easier to build more reliable,
      secure software.  I am interested in programming languages,
      software engineering, and security. I am a member
      of <a href="https://tupl.cs.tufts.edu">TuPL</a>.

      </p>
<p>
      
      Previously, I was a Professor in the <a href="http://www.cs.umd.edu">Department of Computer Science</a>
      and <a href="http://www.umiacs.umd.edu">UMIACS</a> at the <a href="http://www.umd.edu">University of Maryland, College
      Park</a>, as well as a member of <a href="http://www.cs.umd.edu/projects/PL">PLUM</a>.

      </p>

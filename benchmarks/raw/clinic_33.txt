<p>Here at Comprehensive Pain Clinic of Portland we value your overall well-being. Our goal
                                is to give you exceptional care at an affordable price. We strive to care about each
                                patient’s wellness like that of our own. We will do our best to work with you regardless
                                of your budget. This is why we have several different options regarding payment for
                                services rendered. Please call <a href="tel:(503) 244-3389" style="white-space:nowrap">(503)

                                chiropractic visits or for a complete list of our payment options. We are happy to
                                answer your questions!</p>
<p><strong>Insurance </strong><br/>
                                Here at Comprehensive Pain Clinic of Portland we accept most insurance plans as well as
                                auto accidents, worker’s compensation, and personal injury cases resulting in back pain,
                                headaches or other related conditions. Feel free to call our office at <a href="tel:(503) 244-3389" style="white-space:nowrap">(503) 244-3389</a> for any
                                additional questions about your health insurance coverage. </p>
<p><strong>Family Plans</strong><br/>
                                Here at Comprehensive Pain Clinic of Portland we are dedicated to you and your family’s
                                health. We are happy to customize a plan that suits your family’s financial and health
                                needs. It is always easier to adapt a health mind-set together than alone. We want to be
                                a part of your wellness lifestyle every step of the way. Contact our office today at <a href="tel:(503) 244-3389" style="white-space:nowrap">(503) 244-3389</a> to start
                                this journey together.</p>
<p><strong>Health on a Budget/Wellness Plans</strong><br/>
                                Here at Comprehensive Pain Clinic of Portland, we want you to know we care. Our goal is
                                to help you feel better regardless of what your situation entails. Prevention is always
                                the best choice; however, we know most people realize this after the injury or pain
                                persists. Regular chiropractic visits are much less time-consuming in the long run than
                                invasive surgeries or other pain management options after an injury. We encourage you to
                                take your health seriously, and call us today at <a href="tel:(503) 244-3389" style="white-space:nowrap">(503)
                                    244-3389</a> to start your journey. We want to help you regardless of your budget.

<p>UCLA Health is proud to serve Beverly Hills and the surrounding communities.  Our
                                Beverly Hills office on Wilshire Blvd. is a multi-specialty office consisting of general
                                Internal Medicine and Pediatrics, as well as specialists in the following disciplines:
                                Allergy &amp; Immunology, Cardiology, Cardiac Imaging, Dermatology, Endocrinology,
                                Pulmonology and Rheumatology. All of the physicians in the group are board
                                certified in their fields. </p>
<p>All are UCLA Medical Group members accepting most insurances and hold faculty teaching
                                appointments in the David Geffen School of Medicine and are on staff at <a href="https://www.uclahealth.org/Santa-Monica">UCLA Health - Santa Monica
                                    Medical Center</a> and <a href="https://www.uclahealth.org/Reagan">Ronald
                                    Reagan UCLA Medical Center</a>.</p>
<p>We offer exemplary medical care, delivered with outstanding customer service throughout
                                each visit, starting at the front desk. We are available to see our patients for same
                                day appointments for illnesses.</p>
<h2>Services at a glance</h2>
<ul>
<li>Well Care for Adults</li>
<li>Well Woman Care</li>
<li>Pediatric care (newborn to adolescent)</li>
<li>Same Day Sick Appointments</li>
<li>Care of the hospitalized patient</li>
<li>Coordination of care for patients with chronic medical conditions and/or multiple
                                    specialist physicians
                                </li>
</ul>
